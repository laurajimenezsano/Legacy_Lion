The Root of Legacy Lion

According to the US Department of State, "the United States hosts more immigrants than any other country, with more than one million people arriving every year as permanent legal residents, asylum-seekers and refugees".

Even within the United States, in 2019 approximately 7.4 million people relocated within different states. Relocation within the United States has increased by 9% since 2010.

Some of the main drivers of relocation are:

  • Economic Strength
  • Healthcare
  • Education

    • "Migration is an expression of the human aspiration for dignity, safety and a better future. It is part of the social fabric, part of our very make-up as a human family" - Bank Ki-moon

    Why Legacy Lion?

    Legacy Lion is a tool that helps family connect and tell their own story for their future generation.

    As a user, you can post your milestones, such as the birth of a new child, your relocation to a new country, college graduation, among many other.

    Different to other social media platforms, where a user needs to accept another [unless kept in public view, which we do not recommend], in this application you can become a proxy for your ancestors. Before your ancestors pass, they can make you a proxy of their profile. This means, that they can inherit your posts, images, and complete hierarchy. That way, your children, grandchildren and even 400 years of generations after can continue to see the legacy.
